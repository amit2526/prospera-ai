Process

From Cold Lead to
Closed Deal

Seven specialized AI agents — including a Human Supervisor — each mastering one stage of your pipeline, working in perfect sync around the clock.

AGENT 01
The Prospector
Scours Google Maps, LinkedIn, Google Ads, Meta Ads, email inboxes, and business directories to find prospects matching your Ideal Customer Profile — extracting contact details into a clean, deduplicated CRM list automatically.
Lead Discovery
🔍
📡
AGENT 02
The Outreacher
Sends personalized first-touch messages across WhatsApp, email, and Telegram — referencing each prospect's business, industry pain points, and optimal timing. Manages multi-channel follow-ups automatically until there's a reply.
Multi-Channel Outreach
AGENT 03
The Engager
When a prospect replies — on WhatsApp, email, or Telegram — this agent takes over. It understands context across channels, answers questions, handles objections, shares case studies, and books a discovery call directly in your calendar.
Multi-Channel Engagement
🧠
📞
AGENT 04
The Caller
Conducts AI-powered voice discovery calls — understanding the prospect's needs, budget, and timeline. Recommends the right package or escalates to the Human Supervisor for complex or high-value deals.
Voice Qualification
AGENT 05
The Supervisor
When a deal requires human judgment — complex requirements, high-value accounts, or sensitive objections — the Supervisor steps in. They receive a full brief with conversation history, call transcript, lead score, and a recommended next action. Once resolved, the pipeline resumes automatically.
Human-in-the-Loop
👤
📋
AGENT 06
The Closer
The moment a prospect says yes, a branded proposal PDF and payment invoice are delivered to their inbox — with a "Pay Now" button. Stripe and Razorpay integrated. Follow-ups handled automatically.
Proposals & Invoicing
AGENT 07
The Dispatcher
On payment confirmation, a project ticket is auto-created in Jira, Trello, or ClickUp — populated with the client's details, requirements from the call transcript, and timeline. Your delivery team starts the same day.
Project Kickoff
