7-Agent Architecture

Meet Your AI Sales Team

Seven specialized agents, each with a distinct role, sharing context seamlessly — so every interaction builds on the last. A human supervisor steps in whenever the situation demands it.

AGENT_01
🔍
The Prospector
Data Scraping & Lead Discovery
Scours Google Maps, LinkedIn, Google Ads, Meta Ads, email inboxes, business directories, and industry databases to find businesses matching your Ideal Customer Profile — extracting names, phones, emails, websites, and location data into a structured CRM list.
Example: Pulls warm leads from your Google Ads audience, Meta lead forms, and cold prospects from Google Maps — all unified into a single deduplicated list overnight.
AGENT_02
📡
The Outreacher
Multi-Channel First-Touch Outreach
Initiates personalized outreach across WhatsApp, email, and Telegram — referencing each prospect's business name, industry, and a specific pain point. Sends in smart batches, respects timing windows, and manages follow-ups automatically across all channels.
Example: Sends a cold email, follows up on WhatsApp 24 hours later, and drops a Telegram message if no response — all with the same personalized context, zero manual effort.
AGENT_03
🧠
The Engager
Multi-Channel Conversation & Booking
When a prospect replies — on WhatsApp, email, or Telegram — this agent takes over. It understands context across channels, answers questions, handles objections, and guides the conversation toward booking a discovery call. Shares links, brochures, and calendar slots within the same thread.
Example: Prospect replies on Telegram asking about cost → agent responds with packages, shares a portfolio link, and suggests three available time slots — all without switching platforms.
AGENT_04
📞
The Caller
AI Voice Discovery & Qualification
Places an AI-powered voice call at the scheduled time, conducting a structured discovery conversation: understanding needs, budget, timeline, and goals — then recommending a package or escalating to the Human Supervisor for complex deals.
Example: Asks about online presence and goals, then recommends "Premium Website + Online Ordering" — or flags the deal for human review if the budget or scope is outside standard parameters.
AGENT_05
👤
The Supervisor
Human-in-the-Loop Oversight
When a deal requires human judgment — complex requirements, high-value accounts, sensitive objections, or edge cases — the Supervisor steps in. They receive a full brief: conversation history, call transcript, lead score, and recommended next action. Once resolved, the pipeline resumes automatically.
Example: A prospect requests a custom enterprise integration. The Supervisor is notified with a full brief, jumps in to negotiate, then hands back to the Closer once terms are agreed.
AGENT_06
📋
The Closer
Proposal, Invoice & Confirmation
Once the prospect says yes, immediately generates a professional email with a proposal summary, pricing breakdown, and payment invoice — integrated with Stripe or Razorpay. Handles follow-ups on unpaid invoices.
Example: Within 60 seconds of verbal confirmation, prospect receives a branded proposal PDF with a "Pay Now" button.
AGENT_07
🚀
The Dispatcher
Internal Handoff & Project Kickoff
Upon payment confirmation, automatically creates a project ticket in Jira, Trello, Asana, or ClickUp — with all client details, discovery call transcript requirements, selected package, and timeline. Delivery team is notified instantly.
Example: Jira ticket created: "New Project — Bella's Italian Kitchen — Premium Website — Due: 3 weeks — Requirements: [auto-populated]"
